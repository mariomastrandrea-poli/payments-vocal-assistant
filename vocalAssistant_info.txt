Types of user intents:

0) none

1) Check Balance
- Currency (Symbol or name)
- account (default, primary or bank name)

2) Check transactions
- Account (default/primary or the name of the bank)
- Name (surname, last name or full name, common name of relative) 

3) Send money / 4) Request money
- Amount (Decimal number + currency)
- Name (surname, last name or full name, common name of relative)
- Account (default/primary or the name of the bank)

5) yes

6) no


-----------
Types of Named Entities:

0) O (out)
(No entity)

1) B-AMOUNT
2) I-AMOUNT
(Amount value made of currency (literal or symbol) + sum (with or without decimal part, literal or numeric)

3) B-BANK
4) I-BANK
(Name of the bank, or primary/default one)

5) B-CURRENCY
6) I-CURRENCY
(Just the currency, literal or symbol)

7) B-USER
8) I-USER  
(Full name or just first name, or common name + first name)
